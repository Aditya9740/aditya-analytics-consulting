Sales Pipeline Analytics & Revenue Optimization
① Problem Statement
B2B sales teams had no visibility into where deals were leaking

Sales managers were working blind — no way to see which pipeline stages were losing deals, which regions underperformed, or why high-value opportunities were lost more than smaller ones. All data sat in disconnected spreadsheets with no unified view.

② Before — Raw Data State
❌ Messy CRM data
1,000+ records with duplicates, wrong stage names, missing values
❌ No pipeline metrics
Win rate, deal velocity, revenue forecast — all calculated manually
❌ Zero regional insight
No way to compare North vs South vs East vs West performance
❌ No reporting system
Weekly reports took 3+ hours to compile manually every time
③ Process — What I Did
🐍
STEP 1
Data Generation
Python · Faker · pandas — 1,000+ synthetic CRM records
🧹
STEP 2
Data Cleaning
45 duplicates removed, 23 stage variants standardised
🗄
STEP 3
SQL Analysis
PostgreSQL queries — win rate, velocity, revenue by region
📊
STEP 4
Power BI Dashboard
6 visuals, 8 DAX measures, interactive filters
④ Before vs After — Comparison
METRIC ❌ BEFORE ✅ AFTER
Data QualityDuplicates, nulls, 23 inconsistent stage names100% clean, standardised, analysis-ready dataset
Reporting Time3+ hours manual work per weekly reportUnder 5 minutes — automated Power BI refresh

Win Rate InsightUnknown — no calculation in place63% overall · Large deal gap found (₹1.83L lost vs ₹1.24L won)

Vendor Spend Intelligence & Cost Optimization System
① Problem Statement
Companies spending crores had no idea where the money was going

Finance and procurement teams were managing ₹41+ Cr of vendor spend across 20 suppliers and 10 departments with zero visibility into concentration risk, overspending categories, or negotiation leverage. Cost savings were left on the table year after year.

② Before — Raw Data State
❌ Unstructured spend data
582 transactions in raw CSV — no categorisation or vendor scoring
❌ No vendor benchmarking
No way to compare vendors on price, reliability, or risk
❌ Budget leakage unknown
Departments overspending — nobody knew which or by how much
❌ No savings strategy
No data to justify vendor renegotiation or consolidation
③ Process — What I Did
🐍
STEP 1
Data Generation
582 synthetic Indian B2B transactions — 20 vendors, 10 depts, 2 years
🧹
STEP 2
Data Cleaning
Standardised vendor names, fixed nulls, categorised spend by type
🗄
STEP 3
SQL Analysis
12 SQL queries — spend concentration, vendor scoring, budget variance
📊
STEP 4
Power BI Dashboard
Vendor risk matrix, spend heatmap, cost savings scenario model
④ Before vs After — Comparison
METRIC ❌ BEFORE ✅ AFTER
Spend VisibilityNo category or vendor-level breakdown₹41.54Cr fully mapped — by vendor, dept, category, month
Vendor RiskUnknown — no concentration analysisTop 3 vendors = 42% spend flagged as high concentration risk

Cost SavingsNo savings strategy or data to support it₹2.1 Cr annualised savings identified — 7–8% on total spend
